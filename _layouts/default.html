Research

My research centers on computer vision and multimodal learning for human-centered intelligent systems. I build models that reason over visual and temporal context and develop evaluation methods that improve robustness and interpretability in safety-critical settings, including autonomous driving. Currently, I am particularly interested in vision-language supervision and generalizable benchmarks for end-to-end autonomy and human–AI interaction.
