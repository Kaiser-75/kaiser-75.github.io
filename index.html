About Me

I am a final year (L4/T1) undergraduate student at Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology where I am studying Civil Engineering with a major in Transportation Engineering. I am interested in 'Travel Behavior' and 'Intelligent Transport System(ITS)'.

My current research focuses on 'Travel Behavior and Passive Data' under the supervision of Prof. Dr. Annesha Enam and Prof. Dr. Samiul Hasan. It is not published yet. I am conducting my undergraduate thesis in the field of Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITS) under the guidance of Prof. Dr. Moazzem Hossain.

Apart from my academic activities, I really enjoy spending time outdoors and one of my big dreams is to visit as many countries as I can. During my undergrad life, I had the opportunity to visit India with my friends. We explored diverse places such as Kolkata, Darjeeling, Meghalaya, Sikkim and Shillong during these memorable trips! During my free time, I like to go to the gym to stay fit. I also enjoy hanging out with friends and family and I'm always excited to try new types of food when I get the chance.
